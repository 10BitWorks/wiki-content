Issue: Access Constraint — Broken Rolling Door Motor and Asset Obsolescence

Status: RESOLVED | Severity: Medium (Operational Obstruction)

Description

The motor that operates the facility's large rolling industrial door is malfunctioning. Specifically, the automatic limit switches have failed, meaning the motor does not stop when the door reaches its maximum height. This has created a constant risk of the motor burning out or damaging the door track.

Evidence & Verification

### Operational Failure (June 7, 2025)

Link: Hunter Wright reported that the door motor failed to stop, forcing members to manually intervene.

### Documentation (March 2026)

Transcript Quote: "Likely burnt out... it had a tendency to keep running after it went up to max height. I should remind members to hit stop on it, otherwise it keeps running." [Eric Spehr, #general, March 17, 2026]

Field Recording Confirmation: During the walkthrough, members noted: "please do not let anything lean on the sensor." [April 19, 2026]

Maintenance & Operational Friction

In 2025, Connor repaired the door, and it has been mostly working since then.
