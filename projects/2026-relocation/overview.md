# Project Constraints & Requirements

## Moving Strategy
- **Target Location:** The board has selected the **"Botts"** location (8610 Botts Ln) as the primary choice.
- **Legal Strategy Shift (May 2026):** Based on legal counsel, the Board has abandoned the "needy tenant" negotiation strategy. Counsel advises that the Landlord (Alex) is already in material breach of the lease.
- **Goal:** Execute a "Quiet Exit" from the Lachapelle location.
- **Defensibility:** Maintain the "War Chest" of documentation (Issues folder) as evidence to defend against any future claims of lease liability (~$40,000).

## Lachapelle Departure Context (Alex Bahrami)
- **Zero Interaction Policy:** Stop all follow-up on the Transition Proposal and do not send formal repair requests.
- **Evidence for Discovery:** Photographic and video evidence of the **May 2, 2026 Record Flood** (13-year high) is critical. The flood reached nearly to the front door because Property Manager Ryan removed the flashing/shielding.
- **Key Issues for Defense:**

    - **Exterior Shell:** "Swiss cheese" siding and unsealed gaps.
    - **Active Interference:** Blocked water heater shed (prevents maintenance) and "daisy-chained" neighbor sewage.
    - **Asset Loss:** 20+ LED fixtures trapped in neighbor's space; equipment/ladder stolen by contractor "Luis" in March 2026.
    - **Financial Adjustments:** Board is deducting **$2,000** ($1,000/mo for 2 months) from remaining rent to recover unpaid promised sponsorship funds.

## Botts Ln Lease Negotiation (8610 Botts Ln)
- **Current Terms:** 9,900 sq ft, 63-month term, 3 months free base rent, ~$2.96/sq ft NNN charges.
- **Sustainability Risk:** Expected monthly cost (Base + NNN) is **~$7,805** starting Month 4. Current membership intake is **~$6,100–$6,400**. Growth is required.
- **Non-Negotiable Redlines (Sam L. Selig, Broker):**
    - **Technical Autonomy:** Strike the **Modern Edge Security** vendor monopoly. 10BitWorks must maintain its own CCTV (Frigate), network infrastructure, and custom Access Control.
    - **Liability Protection:** Strike the **Rent Acceleration** clause and the **Landlord's Lien** (to protect member-owned tools).
    - **Administrative Fairness:** Extend all notice/cure periods from 10 to **30 days**; require **Email Notice** to be effective; reduce the $100 check fee to actual cost + $25.
    - **HVAC Autonomy:** Since 10BitWorks assumes 100% replacement liability, it must have the right to perform its own internal maintenance (filters/coils).
