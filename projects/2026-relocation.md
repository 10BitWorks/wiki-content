# 2026 Relocation

Project to identify, evaluate, and move 10BitWorks to a new facility. This page is a public summary; detailed documents (lease analysis, meeting notes, repair records, and financials) are private to the organization.

## Goals

- Resolve ongoing facility issues at the current Lachapelle location
- Identify a suitable long-term home for the makerspace
- Manage the transition with minimal disruption to members
